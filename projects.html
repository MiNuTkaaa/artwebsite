The Friendship Park, Khimki. Place that brings not only friends together.

Khimki - the urban town next to Moscow. The Friendship part right in the heart of this city connects people, creatures around and the Mother Nature together. This week's project explores what one feels when enters this place - from the calmness as a still pond to the anxiety after getting disturbed by the sudden crow.
